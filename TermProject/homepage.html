Mission Statement

To serve great crepes to everyone around Ontario. We want to be the best creperie in Canada.

Owner Profile

Steve Smith is a passionate chef with over 10 years of experience in the culinary industry. He has a deep love for crepes and has dedicated his career to perfecting the art of crepe-making. Steve believes that food should not only be delicious but also an experience to remember. His goal is to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where customers can enjoy their meals and create lasting memories.
